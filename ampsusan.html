Amplifying Susan

A communication platform that captures non-verbal cues and represents them through sensory feeback .

The best technology gets out of the way, is simple and unobtrusive when used.

As emerging interfaces demand our sight and attention, the simple act of adjusting the volume of a sound system often requires a concering amount of attention. Our eyes are needed to operate any touchscreen, and often we need to conjure a passcode.

This functional coffee table aims to address these issues with an non-interruptive way to make audio adjustments, while at the same time allowing multiple users to change the volume to comfortable levels without gatekeeping.
