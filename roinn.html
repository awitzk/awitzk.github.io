Roinn

Focusing on the interaction between riders, this shared vehicle aims to create a seamless experience while keeping users safe and comfortable .

Roinn: [Scots Gaelic] to share.

While many in vehicle design are looking at the technical aspect, we approached the issue of reducing the vehicle miles travelled by focusing on providing users with an accessible, safe, private and comfortable experience. With plenty of storage and a dedicated mobile application, this service can break down barriers in the field of shared transport
